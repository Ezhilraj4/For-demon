Nithya Sri

This is not a contract written by fate.

This is a promise written by choice.

I never expected my heart

to find peace in your name.

Nithya Sri,

I promise to stay —

in silence and in smiles.

Even if the world changes,

my feelings for you will not.

This promise has no end date,

only endless moments with you.

— Ezhil

Not because of fate… but because of love.
